POLICY ID: DATA-AI-001
TITLE: Prohibition on Vendor AI Training and Commercialization of Customer Data
SEVERITY: HARD REJECT

Company Policy:
The company must reject any contract that permits a vendor or its affiliates to use
Customer Data, confidential information, prompts, inputs, outputs, or derived data
to train, improve, develop, market, sell, or commercialize the vendor's general
models, products, or services.

Required Acceptable Position:
Customer Data, prompts, inputs, and outputs may be processed only to provide,
secure, support, and maintain the contracted service for the customer. Any other
use requires the customer's prior explicit written opt-in consent.

Decision Rule:
If the contract grants the vendor general AI-training, product-improvement, or
commercialization rights over Customer Data, prompts, outputs, or derived data,
the decision is REJECT. This rule cannot be downgraded to medium risk or approved
through ordinary commercial negotiation. The prohibited language must be removed
or replaced before the contract can proceed.
